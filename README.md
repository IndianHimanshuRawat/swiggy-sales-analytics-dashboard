# Swiggy Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard developed to analyze Swiggy sales performance, customer ordering behavior, food category preferences, and regional sales trends.

The dashboard provides a centralized view of key business metrics, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Food delivery companies generate large volumes of transaction data. Analyzing this data is essential for understanding customer behavior, tracking sales performance, and identifying growth opportunities.

This project aims to transform sales data into meaningful insights through an interactive dashboard.

---

## Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance.
* Analyze customer ordering trends.
* Evaluate customer ratings.
* Identify top-performing states.
* Compare food category performance.
* Support business decisions with data visualization.

## Key Insights

* Generated over ₹53 Million in total sales.
* Processed more than 197K customer orders.
* Maintained an average customer rating of 4.34.
* Vegetarian orders contributed the majority of total sales.
* Karnataka emerged as the highest-performing state.
* Weekend sales outperformed weekday sales.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Increase promotional campaigns during low-performing weekdays.
* Focus expansion efforts on high-performing regions.
* Strengthen partnerships with popular food categories.
* Continuously monitor customer satisfaction metrics.
